[title]
Colleague Face

[description]
A render of a scene from my Trepidation project. Sculpted manually with 99% procedural materials (the LED panel wall in the back uses an image texture. That's it).

This piece involved use of almost every tab within the blender suite, which seriously tested my familarities and capability within the software. Although procedural, I still had to work with UVs on the character's skin and undershirt. The seam edges of the undershirt were done with geometry nodes, as well. 

2nd image excludes the graphics.

3rd image was the initial render, for the sake of framing.

4th video was a lackluster animation I whipped up a while later. Because the character was sculpted, I couldn't animate them at all; I had no further expectations and moved on to new projects.
