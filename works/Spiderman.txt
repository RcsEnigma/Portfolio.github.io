[title]
Spiderman 2099

[description]
Made a week before the release of Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse, because I liked the concept of the character. I knew nothing about him expect that he was probably the villian. After coming out of the theatre seeing him in action, I noticed about a dozen inaccuracies with my depiction of him. But, I never brought myself to alter the final piece.

The hexagons in the background were made in blender, the lots of filters and effects and edits were made to match it with the subject. I think it help sell the movie's aesthetic of 2D and 3D styles clashing.
