[title]
Winged Scientist

[description]
A non-canon depiction of a character from my Trepidation project.

This artwork originally existed only as linework from a few years prior. I felt like drawing without wanting to think of a pose so I dug this up, dusted it off, and cleaned it up.

The cityscape in the background was made in blender with a shader to simulate black outlines. After rendering, I did a lot of work to fix the colors and materials to have it fit in with the rest of the scene. The rings around the planet were also rendered in 3D, but as a separate layer from the cityscape. Everything else was done in 2D.

The entire artwork, from start to finish, was inspired by 'Reaper Hank' by Rhunyc on Newgrounds.
