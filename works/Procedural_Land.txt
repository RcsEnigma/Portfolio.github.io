Procedural Land

[description]
A messy attempt at a vast expanse. The ground is a single material with a thouroughly subdivided plane. The clouds are also procedural.

The 2nd image was my first render. Had to play around with a lot of the values to get something I was happy with, It also lacks any post-processing.

I forsee a remake of this shader material, something far more elaborate which could account for multiple biome types.
